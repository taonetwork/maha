The Simplified Wealth of Nations by Adam Smith


We simplify The Wealth of Nations by removing redundant statements and by organizing Smith's ideas into bullet points. We retain the paragraph numbers so you can check the original. Pounds and shillings are converted to their pence equivalent for easier comparison and computation.

Book 1-Productivity and the Distribution of Revenue

Productivity is from the division of labor of the earners of rent, wage, and profits
