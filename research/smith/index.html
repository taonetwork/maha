Adam Smith


Smith built on the principles established by David Hume and laid the blueprint for an enlightened social system made up of three parts-- Moral system in the Theory of Moral Sentiments, Justice and Political system in the unfinished Lectures of Jurisprudence which makes up our Supersociology, Economic system in the Wealth of Nations which makes up Pantrynomics
